Product Return & Refund

What is the product return and refund process?

Product return is the process of sending back a product to the seller due to defects, damage, or mismatch with the description. Refund is the process of getting your money back after the return is approved.

How long does the refund process take?

Refunds are usually processed within 7-14 business days after the seller approves the return and receives the product.

What should I do if I have not received my refund?

If you have not received your refund after the expected time, please contact Tokopedia customer service for assistance.
