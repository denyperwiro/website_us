Your Privacy Choices

This Privacy Choices Notice is intended for a United States audience and supplements the HP US Privacy Statement. It informs you of the rights you can exercise in relation to your personal information, including information that may be collected from your online activity, use of devices, and interactions you have with HP offline, such as when you engage with our customer support representatives.

Your personal information may be collected, used, disclosed, or otherwise processed by HP for the purposes of managing your relationship with HP and to better serve you by personalizing your experience and interaction with us. Details about what categories and pieces of personal information we collect about you, how we use the information we collect, where and from whom we obtain it, to whom we disclose, sell, or share your personal information, your respective State rights regarding it, and how you can exercise your rights can be found in our HP US Privacy Statement. Throughout this document references to “we,” “us,” “our,” and “Company” refer to HP branded companies and current and future affiliates (herein "HP").

HP will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights and choices, although some of the functionality and features available on HP Services may change or no longer be available to you as the data might be necessary to provide some functionalities or features.

Throughout this document references to ‘’local law’’ or ‘’applicable law’’ refer to US Federal privacy law, privacy US State law and local law where you reside in the United States.

    How We Manage your Privacy Requests

    Upon receipt of your communication, our representative will contact you within a reasonable time to respond to your questions or concerns. Please refrain from sending us sensitive personal information.

To protect your information, we will ask for a signed permission from you authorizing the other person to submit a request on your behalf. If applicable, we will contact you to confirm that you have provided the authorized agent permission to submit the request before we respond to your authorized agent’s request.

    Timing

    We will acknowledge receipt of your request within 10 days after we receive your request. You will receive our response to your request within 45 days of your request, unless we provide you with notice that it will take more than 45 days to respond (in that case, we won't take more than 90 days to respond).
